Labeling Instructions

Draw polygons around each piece of rooftop HVAC equipment. Ignore rooftops that lie outside of the orange boundary. There are three types of HVAC equipment to label:

  1. Packaged Rooftop Units
    • A box with 1-8 small fans, often offset from the center
    • Most common type of equipment in dataset
  2. Air-Cooled Chillers
    • A box with 4 or more small fans located in the center of the box
    • Usually with visible piping leading into the box
    • Relatively uncommon
  3. Water-Cooled Chillers
    • A box with 1-3 very large fans located in the center of the box
    • Fan blades typically clearly visible
    • Often with visible piping leading into the box
    • May be located on rooftop or on ground next to building
    • Uncommon

Other Notes:

Packaged rooftop units with a single centered fan may look like water-cooled chillers. However, water-cooled chillers are much larger than packaged units and are uncommon in the dataset.
